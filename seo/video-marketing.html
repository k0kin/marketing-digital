Types of Videos Used in Marketing

Types of Videos Used in Marketing


Video marketing is an important part of modern advertising, with a range of different types of videos being used to promote products and services. (One example includes) Explainer videos, which give viewers a quick overview of what the product or service is about. Tutorials are another common type; these provide step-by-step instructions on how to use the product or service.

In addition, there are also 'viral' videos, which aim to be entertaining and encourage viewers to share them with their friends! These can be humorous skits or parodies that relate to the product or company in some way. Then there's interactive video content, such as quizzes and surveys which help customers engage more deeply with the brand.

Moreover, 360 degree video allows users to explore their environment by looking around in all directions - it's great for showcasing products from multiple angles! And lastly we have live streaming video; this gives businesses the opportunity to connect directly with their customers in real time.

Overall, there is a wide range of different types of videos used in marketing - each one having its own advantages and disadvantages depending on your objectives. It's worth taking some time to consider which type(s) would work best for you!
